Suitability Analysis MCE Raster

Social Housing Site Selection,
Co. Cavan

M.Sc. Dissertation

Multi-criteria evaluation suitability raster identifying optimal locations for social housing delivery across County Cavan, Ireland. Composite scores were derived from weighted spatial criteria including proximity to unfinished estates, infrastructure access, and land use constraints.

Tools: ArcGIS · Weighted Overlay · Raster Analysis

Service Area Analysis Network Analysis Series 1 of 4

Cinema Catchments,
Northeastern Ireland — Baseline

M.Sc. Dissertation

Baseline 60-minute drive-time catchment model for existing cinema provision across northeastern Ireland. Establishes service area boundaries before introduction of two proposed venues in Dundalk.

Tools: ArcGIS Network Analyst · Small Area Population Statistics

Scenario Modelling Series 2 of 4

With Marshes Cinema,
Dundalk (Proposed)

M.Sc. Dissertation

Catchment redistribution modelling the effect of a proposed cinema at The Marshes, Dundalk. The new venue draws a distinct catchment from the east Dundalk area, reducing reliance on the existing IMC.

Tools: ArcGIS Network Analyst · Scenario Comparison

Scenario Modelling Series 3 of 4

With Retail Park Cinema,
Dundalk (Proposed)

M.Sc. Dissertation

Alternative scenario introducing a second proposed venue at Dundalk Retail Park. Catchment modelling shows a different spatial footprint compared to the Marshes site, serving a more southerly population.

Tools: ArcGIS Network Analyst · Scenario Comparison

Final Scenario Service Provision Series 4 of 4

With Both Proposed Cinemas,
Northeastern Ireland — Final Scenario

M.Sc. Dissertation

Final scenario modelling simultaneous operation of both proposed Dundalk venues. Combined catchment analysis reveals how the two sites partition demand, the degree of overlap with existing provision, and residual underserved areas beyond 60 minutes of any cinema.

Tools: ArcGIS Network Analyst · Network Dataset · Small Area Statistics

About

About

I'm a GIS professional and cartographer based in Seattle, with over a decade of experience translating complex spatial data into clear, considered maps. My work spans utility infrastructure, transit networks, and editorial print cartography.

I hold an M.Sc. in GIS and Remote Sensing, and have worked across the energy, travel, and mapping sectors — including roles at Puget Sound Energy and Expedia Group, and contract work for Google Maps.
